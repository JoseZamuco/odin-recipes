The Kapampangan Sisig

Ingredients:

  • Pigs face and Ears
  • Onion
  • Calamansi
  • salt and pepper

How to cook:

  1. Boil Pigs face and Ears with salt and aromatics.
  2. Grill Pigs face and Ears till maillard is achieved.
  3. Chop the Grilled Pig and Ears and Onions to tiny pieces
  4. Season the salad with calamansi, salt, and pepper to taste.
