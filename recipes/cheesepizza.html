The Founding Father Cheese Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozarella cheese
  • provolone cheese
  • olive oil
  • semolina

How to cook:

  1. Make pizza dough into flat circle
  2. Put tomato sauce in to pizza dough
  3. Put Mozarella and Provolone cheese
  4. And olive oil on the edge of the pie
  5. Put semolina on pizza board then cook it inside the oven
  6. Slice pizza on to your liking
