Sipo Egg

Ingredients:

  • 2 dozens of Quail Eggs
  • 500ml of All-purpose Cream
  • 2 cups of Mixed Vegetables
  • 1/4 cup of Butter

How to cook:

  1. Boil all Quail Eggs then peel
  2. Put Butter in the pan and stir-fry Mixed Vegetables
  3. Mix the All-purpose Cream
  4. If the veggies are soft, Mix Quail Eggs
  5. Plate and Enjoy!
